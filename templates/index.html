What is PepeVote

PepeVote is a democratized digital art curation platform. Every week, participants in the Rare Pepe economy vote to select two works of art to be permanently added to the collection.

How it works

1. Every week artists submit digital art to the gallery

2. The Rare Pepe community votes on which art to accept

3. Two pieces of art are chosen each week

4. The first piece is chosen by majority vote by owned Pepecash

5. The second piece is chosen by majority vote by owned Pepe cards

6. Details can be found at the docs here
