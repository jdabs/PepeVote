Overview on Voting
Your vote is a rating of how much you like a piece of art, and can be changed once every bitcoin block.
Votes do not get used up when rating, feel free to vote for as many (or as few) submissions as you like.
Votes are calculated, and is art chosen, each week on the first Bitcoin block after Sunday 11:59PM PST.
